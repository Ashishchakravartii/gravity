As a country driven by our people’s energy we realize the potential of interactive learning for increasing student’s technological proficiency along with encouraging teamwork. By introducing new technologies we would like to foster interest in aeromodelling, coding and robotics

Beyond Gravity is an organisation which started as a hobby and now has established itself as a state of the art establishment imparting knowledge and skill development in the field of aeromodelling and drone technology.

We pride ourselves in having a highly qualified team of experts and industry mentors who are engaged in generating interest towards Aviation Technology and robotics through the innovative use of hands-on learning methods.

We deploy various disciplines like Aeromodelling, Drone technology, Robotics, project development and Machine learning to enhance a student’s knowledge base. We offer various training & workshops modules for school and college students and even for hobby enthusiasts.

True learning takes place when an enthusiast has an opportunity to discover on their own, through hands-on & experimental modes. By allowing the learner to be involved in a learning process that excites energies, engages & enriches them to a wide variety of skills & concepts, they will discover a path that will enable them to have a more productive education.

Mission

Accelerate India’s transition into a drone technology hub.

Vision

To make aeromodelling and drone education available to everyone.
