Fixed Wing

Fixed wing UAVs, consists of a rigid wing that has a predetermined airfoil which make flight capable by generating lift caused by the UAVs forward airspeed. This airspeed is generated by forward thrust usually by the means of a propeller being turned by an internal combustion engine or electric motor.

Multirotor

Rotary wing UAVs consist of 4, 6 or 8 rotor blades that revolve around a fixed mast, this is known as a rotor. The biggest advantage of rotary UAVs is the ability to takeoff and land vertically. This allows the user to operate within a smaller vicinity with no substantial landing/take off area required. A complete solution to UAV operations dealing with fixed wing & Multi-rotor.
